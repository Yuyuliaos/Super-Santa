Welcome!

Are you ready to save Christmas by traveling through the mountains of the North Pole?

History

The Grinch has created a virus that has infected Santa's elves, without them there will be no presents and no Christmas!

Don't give up!

Now you have to collect items by traveling through the mountains of the North Pole and throw them at the infected elves to cure them.

Save Christmas!

You have to cure Santa's elves, you can throw the antidote at them and gain points or let them pass and lose points.

Controls

Use the spacebar to jump

Use the enter button to interact

Mint Stick

The Christmas items contain the antidote to cure the elves.

Christmas Ball

When you see one of these items take it and throw it at the elves.
